Privacy Policy — FacelessFactory Analytics

Last updated: 26 June 2026

FacelessFactory Analytics ("the App") is a private, personal analytics tool used by its sole operator to view performance metrics of social media accounts that the operator owns and controls. The App is not offered to the public and has no other users.

What data we access

With the account owner's explicit authorization (via TikTok Login), the App reads, through the official TikTok Display API, the following data for the owner's own connected TikTok accounts:

  • Basic profile information (user.info.basic): display name, avatar, open ID.
  • The owner's own public videos and their public metrics (video.list): view count, like count, comment count, share count, title and publish date.

How we use it

The data is used solely to display the owner's own content performance in a private dashboard, so the owner can see which of their own videos perform best. The data is never sold, shared, transferred, or used for advertising, profiling, or any third-party purpose.

Storage and retention

Authorization tokens and fetched metrics are stored locally on the operator's own computer only. Nothing is uploaded to any external server. The operator can revoke access at any time from their TikTok account settings, and may delete all stored data by removing the local files.

Data sharing

No data is shared with any third party. There are no other users, advertisers, or partners.
